About Me

My Story

Hi, its a pleasure to meet you. My name is Roberto and I am a UX-UI designer based out of Rhode Island.

As a first generation American I grew up in a very family oriented household where we were all expected to work together for the good of the family.

This upbringing along with my passion for artistic expression drove me to my career as a Chef, where I was able to rise up through the ranks to hold the title of Executive Chef.

After over a decade in the hospitality industry my Leadership abilities, Problem Solving skills and Resourcefulness are second to none.

I now bring my skills to the digital world with the goal to become a top level designer that will strive to create products that serve and care for user needs all the while pushing the industry standard.
